Compliance & Privacy

Transparency, Integrity, and Security for the Padua Foundation.

1. Privacy Policy & Data Security

At Padua Group LLC, we value your privacy as a sacred trust. This policy explains how we handle your information when you interact with our spiritual resources or contribute to our mission.

Secure Cloud Processing (2FA)

To ensure maximum security, we utilize a "Headless" payment architecture. When you contribute, you are prompted to enter your mobile phone number. A Secure 6-Digit Code is sent via SMS to verify your identity. This information is stored in a secured, encrypted cloud vault managed by our PCI-DSS Level 1 compliant partners (Stripe & Donorbox). We do not store credit card numbers on our local servers.

3. Contribution & Refund Policy

Entity Status: Padua Group LLC is a legal business entity operating as a private foundation. As an LLC, please be advised that contributions are currently not tax-deductible for federal income tax purposes under IRS 501(c)(3) rules, unless specifically noted for a designated non-profit project.

Refunds: Because contributions are immediately deployed to support our global ministry and operational costs, all gifts are considered final. If an error was made in the amount entered, please contact us within 24 hours at b1wgod@live.com.
